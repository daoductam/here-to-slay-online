# Audio Assets Directory

Please place the following `.mp3` audio files in this directory:

## Background Music (BGM)
- `bgm_lobby.mp3`: Cozy, light fantasy tavern style music (played in the lobby).
- `bgm_gameplay.mp3`: Epic/adventurous strategy style music (played during normal gameplay).
- `bgm_battle.mp3`: Intense battle music (played during challenge and roll phases).
- `bgm_victory.mp3`: Fanfare/victorious music (played when the game ends).

## Sound Effects (SFX)
- `sfx_dice.mp3`: Sound of dice rolling.
- `sfx_draw.mp3`: Sound of drawing/sliding a card.
- `sfx_play.mp3`: Sound of playing a card.
- `sfx_challenge.mp3`: Sudden epic impact/war horn for challenge trigger.
- `sfx_slay.mp3`: Sword slash / monster death cry for slaying a monster.
- `sfx_chat.mp3`: Subtle notification alert sound for incoming chat messages.
